Diet basics — consistent all the way through
Target: ~1,900–2,100 kcal/day. ~500 kcal deficit = ~2 kg/month from diet alone, more from workouts.

Eat more: Rice / roti + dal + sabzi, eggs (3–4/day), curd, seasonal fruits, nuts, 3+ litres water daily.
Cut out: Maida, fried snacks, cold drinks, sugar in chai, late-night eating.
Protein goal: ~130–150g/day — eggs, paneer, dal, chicken or fish if non-veg.
Key rule: Finish dinner by 8:30 PM. Don't eat after. This single habit moves the needle more than any single workout.
